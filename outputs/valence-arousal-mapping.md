### Visualization Description
Imagine a line graph drawn over the four quadrants of the valence-arousal plane:

1.  **Start:** The line originates in the upper left quadrant, representing high stress due to the war.
2.  **Middle:** It swings up into the upper right quadrant as the speaker begins discussing their creative work.
3.  **End:** The path gradually migrates towards the lower, more neutral-to-reflective sections of the grid, ending in a state of weary introspection. The overall movement is a zigzagging path between high-intensity states caused by the external war environment and low-intensity, intellectual states triggered by the speaker’s personal hobbies.
